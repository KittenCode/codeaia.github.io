Kitten Chess


--This is an implementation of Chess Game, developed by Kittencode. This game is written on Java, with the help of slick2d 2D game library.

--Kitten chess is just another chess game. The difference is, it is written in slick2d, an anyone who wants to program games in 2-dimensions can easily dive in this code.

--Kitten Chess is not available to play for now, since it is under development. But our aim is to develop an AI and make it playable for two human on the same computer.
